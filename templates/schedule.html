Schedule

THE WEDDING DAY

1pm - Welcome Drinks & A Very Special Welcome from Pisco

2pm - The Ceremony begins - It's go time! Kindly be seated by 1:45 to start on time.

2:20pm and onwards - After the ceremony the celebration will continue with drinks, dinner and plenty of dancing.

7/7:30pm - Evening Guests Arrive - Let the fiesta continue!

11:30pm - Carriages - time to head home after an unforgettable time.

A friendly reminder to pre-book your taxi - your future self will thank you at 11:30pm!

There are two main entrances to the venue, and signs will be in place to direct drivers to the correct drop-off point.

THE DAY AFTER

Coffee and farewell hugs at the venue from 11pm for anyone who happens to be passing by on their way home - a lovely chance to say thank you and goodbye before everyone heads off.

Parking will be available on this day.
